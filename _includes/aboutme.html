About me

Hi, guys! I am currently a master student in computer science at UIUC. Here I worked with data mining group supervised by Prof. Jiawei Han Previously, I worked as a research assistant in State Key Laboratory of CAD & CG, under the supervision of Prof. Deng Cai. My graduate research focus on weakly supervised information extraction such as Relation Extraction, Open Information Extraction and Open Event Extraction. Now I am working on heterogenous information network embedding over constructed networks. Some interesting applications can be event prediction, fraud detection, etc. Overall, I would love to mine structured and unstructured data from web scale data.
